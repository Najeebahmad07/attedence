Instructions for Teachers

Please follow the instructions below:

  1. Record Attendance: Record attendance for each class session promptly. Use the official attendance sheets provided by the college.
  2. Marking System: Use a clear and consistent system for marking attendance, such as P (Present), A (Absent), L (Late), or E (Excused). Avoid marking attendance as blank or leaving it unmarked.
  3. Late Arrivals: Clearly define the cut-off time for marking students as late. Note that students arriving after this time should be marked as absent, unless they provide a valid excuse.
  4. Excused Absences: Accept excused absences only with valid reasons. Students providing legitimate excuses (medical certificates, official documents) should be marked as excused.
  5. Notification: Notify the college administration immediately if you encounter any irregularities or challenges in attendance management.
  6. Makeup Classes: Report any makeup classes or changes in the schedule to the administrative office promptly to ensure accurate attendance tracking.
  7. Documentation: Keep accurate and up-to-date records of attendance. This documentation may be requested for verification purposes.
  8. Communication: Communicate attendance policies clearly to students at the beginning of the semester and make them aware of the consequences of poor attendance.

Thank you for your cooperation in maintaining accurate attendance records. If you have any questions or concerns, please contact the college administration.
